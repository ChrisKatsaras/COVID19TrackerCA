Sources

How We Get Our Vaccination Data

Our COVID-19 vaccination data is manually compiled exclusively from government sources in near real-time, as new information is released.

For the moment, there's still much variability in where data is released day-to-day. Numbers may be shared in press conferences, interviews, press releases, online government dashboards, situation reports or even through direct correspondence with provincial governments. However, we only report official data that's been directly shared by provincial governments.

As vaccination reporting becomes more standardized among the provinces, we will post a standard sources list for all provinces as we do for our other data.

For now, we're sharing a sample list of sources that we have used for each province to paint a picture of the types of data sources that we rely on:

  • Quebec: Dashboard
  • Ontario: Situation Reports
  • Alberta: Press Conferences
  • British Columbia: Press Releases
  • Manitoba: Press Releases
  • Saskatchewan: Press Releases
  • Nova Scotia: Press Conferences
  • New Brunswick: Press Releases
  • Newfoundland and Labrador: Press Conferences
  • Prince Edward Island: Press Conferences
